Project features

Our project has the purpose of saving time and money in the long term. One of its most outstanding characteristics is that it cuts curves in a precise and exact way, since it is controlled by a program, whether autocad or other, the precision and accuracy of This machine will facilitate many things, such as: small pieces in wood, paper, or other.

The CO2 source laser is the one most commonly used. It allows to cut many more materials and at a higher speed than the pulsed laser. It is considered more reliable, efficient, robust and easy to operate than all other lasers. A laser cutter is a machine that allows cutting or recording with the help of a control command from a 2D program. A laser launched at high power concentrates on a fragile surface and thus manages to cut or engrave. This project has little demand since, buying a laser cutter costs; For that reason we decided to elaborate this machine but for the reach of the buyer, we saw the statistics and the stable statement for both us and the buyer. We also have a small circuit that will help us in our digital circuit design.
